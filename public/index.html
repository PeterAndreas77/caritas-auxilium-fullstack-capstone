App Features

image from unsplash

Convenience

Caritas Auxilium saves confirmation numbers of your donations and charity names, so you do not have to remember them or have the risk of losing them. Furthermore, this App makes a report of your donations per year. Easy to print and you can use the report summary to fill into your tax deductions.

Robust

Caritas Auxilium uses the latest crisis reports taken straight from Relief Web API, which is a public API for humanitarian crisis and disasters maintained by United Nations. As a result, the App uses a robust search algorithm so you can search by terms without modifying them. Of course, the algorithm is not a wizard or a magician, so make sure you have the right spelling when you want to look for something.

Language

Caritas Auxilium means something entirely different in Latin. It's not just the name of an App. Caritas means "the virtue of altruistic love", and Auxilium means "to help or to relief". So, combined they meaning will be the altruistic love to help or relief people.

How To Use

image from unsplash

  1. You must register or login to use this app.
  2. Click register on the header to be taken to the register page. You will be prompted to fill out a registration form.
  3. Click login on the header to be taken to the login page. You will be asked to enter your username and password.
  4. Once You registered or logged in, you will be inside the main user page, which is populated with the latest ten crisis from around the globe.
  5. Click the link on any crisis card to read more of the crisis that interest you.
  6. Click the donate button on the crisis card to donate to that crisis. You are then prompted to fill out a form to keep a record of your donation.
  7. Clicking the cancel button will return you to the recent crisis page.
  8. You can also search for any crisis using any search term. The result is limited to ten crisis.
  9. The crisis you added are located on my crisis.
  10. You can look at a list of your donations when you click my donations.
  11. Click update to update your chosen donation.
  12. Click delete to delete your unwanted donation.
  13. You can search for a specific donation using the search bar.
  14. my account contain the details of your account. You can change your first name, last name, and email address, nothing more.
  15. You can look at your donation activities throughout a certain year by clicking my reports. Once you are inside the page, you can pull report from the server.
