App Features

First Feature. This App saves confirmation number of your donations, so you do not have to remember or having the risk of losing them in the laundry bin

Second Feature. This App uses the latest and most recent crisis from Relief Web API, which is a public API for humanitarian crisis and disasters maintained by United Nations.

Third Feature. This App uses a robust search algorithm so you can search by terms without modifying them. Of course, the algorithm is not a wizard, so make sure you have the right spelling.

Fourth Feature. This App can make a report of your donations per year. Easy to print and You can use them to fill your tax deductions.

How To Use

  1. User must register or login to use this app. You have no heart if you don't. There are crisis out there and people need help.
  2. To register, click the link on the landing page header that says register.
  3. To Login, click the link on the landing page header that says login.
  4. Once You register or logged if, you will be in the main user page. Menu is hidden at the top, while your main page is populated with the latest ten crisis from around the globe.
  5. Click the link on each card to read more of the crisis.
  6. You can then choose the crisis you deemed worthy. The donate is located below the crisis.
  7. If you don't think people need help, you can cancel the crisis to go back to the latest crisis, using the cancel button.
  8. You can also search for any crisis using any search term. The result is limited to ten.
  9. Your chosen crisis are located on my crisis.
  10. You can search for a specific crisis you have chosen by using the search bar.
  11. To donate, click the donate button on the crisis card.
  12. You are then prompted to fill out a form to keep a record of your donation. (Unfortunately, there is no charity API with a robust search features, so you have to do your own googling to find your own charity to donate to the chosen crisis)
  13. Again, if you don't want to donate to that crisis, you can click the cancel button, conveniently located to the donatebutton.
  14. You can look at a list of your donations when you click my donations.
  15. You can search for a specific donation using the search bar.
  16. my account contain the details of your account. You can change your first name, last name, and email address, nothing more.
  17. You can look at your donation activities throughout a certain year by clicking my reports . Once you are inside the page, you can pull report from the server.
